Nature and Design

Nature is the ultimate designer, effortlessly combining beauty, function, and balance. From the patterns of a leaf to entire ecosystems, every element serves a purpose and works in harmony. Its designs are timeless, sustainable, and adaptive, teaching us that true design is about both aesthetics and purpose.

About me

Hi, I am Aditya Kulkarni, a design student; currently doing my Bachelors in User Experience design at MIT School of design, Alandi. In my journey till now I have always been the curious one wanting to know the meaning and working of different things. Design is like a mixture of both art and science to me, it is like using creative minds to simplify critical problems.
